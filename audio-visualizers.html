Energy: Global input gain for all visualizers (0-200%).

Particles: Sets how many orbiting particles are simulated (2-64).

Size: Global particle size multiplier for Orbits.

Variation: Blends particle size/mass from default toward each particle's randomized target.

Streaks: Extends orbit trails beyond default length (0% keeps current baseline).

Brightness: Overall visibility for Orbit wallpaper layers and color-shift overlays.

Dynamic: Threshold/sensitivity for bright-layer audio response (higher = stricter).

Time: Global Orbits time scale (50%=1x, left slower, right faster; up to 5x each way).

Initial velocity: Sets particle spawn speed scale from 0x to 50x.

Direction sync: 0% keeps random spawn direction, 100% makes all spawns align to one center-tangential direction.

Zoom: Camera zoom for Orbits (lower values zoom out to fit larger trajectories on screen).

Black holes: Chooses a 1-6 black-hole layout that bends wallpaper light and can capture/respawn particles.

Zoom: Makes the oscilloscope drawing smaller or larger.

Strength: Sets the base frequency for both X and Y axes.

Phase: Sets how fast the shape rotates over time.

Phase response: Lets bass and kick temporarily slow/stop phase rotation.

Bass emphasis: Strengthens low-end influence on retune and triangle-like shaping.

Shape LFO: Modulates Shape around center; Shape sets LFO depth amount.

Fruits: Controls how strongly image 3 appears from high-band activity.

Fruit LFO: Modulates Fruits around center; Fruits sets LFO depth amount.

Rain: Adds slow falling pixel droplets with color morphing and volume dynamics.

Drop Size: Increases rain droplet size from minimum up to around 5x.

Strobe: Sets how much X-axis detune is applied.

Strobe LFO: Adds a repeating modulation to Strobe (slow to fast).

Warp: Pulls the shape toward saw-like behavior from high-frequency detail.

Fold: Adds wave-folding on X and Y; louder moments fold more.

Fold response: 50% is neutral. Left makes low-end reduce fold, right makes high-end reduce fold.

Kick Threshold: Manual peak threshold for tap detection in BPM Lab.
